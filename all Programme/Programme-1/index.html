Essaouira Programme

Morning : Departure from Marrakech

• 7:00 AM: Depart early from Marrakech to make the most of your day in Essaouira. The drive takes about 2.5 to 3 hours (175 km), passing through the scenic Moroccan countryside and argan tree groves.

• 9:30 AM: Arrive in Essaouira and begin exploring the city.

Essaouira Programme

Morning : Explore Essaouira’s Medina

• 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM: Start by exploring Essaouira’s medina, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Wander through its narrow streets, lined with white-washed houses with blue shutters. You can browse the various artisan shops, selling handicrafts, textiles, jewelry, and local artwork.

• 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Visit the Skala de la Ville, a historic sea bastion with impressive views of the Atlantic Ocean and the city’s ramparts. This is a great spot for photography.

Afternoon: Discover Essaouira’s Port and Beach

• 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Head to the bustling port of Essaouira. Watch the fishermen bringing in their daily catch and visit the vibrant fish market. You can also explore the Skala du Port, a fortress offering panoramic views of the harbor and city.

• 1:00 PM - 2:30 PM: Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch at one of the local restaurants near the port or in the medina. Essaouira is known for its delicious and affordable seafood.

Afternoon : Relax and Enjoy the Beach

• 2:30 PM - 4:00 PM: Take a leisurely stroll along Essaouira’s sandy beach, where you can relax, dip your feet in the ocean, or even try windsurfing or kite surfing if you’re feeling adventurous.
The beach is also a great place to enjoy the laid-back atmosphere of Essaouira.

Additional Tips :

• Bring a jacket or windbreaker, as Essaouira can be breezy and cooler than Marrakech.

• Wear comfortable walking shoes, as the medina and port areas are best explored on foot.
