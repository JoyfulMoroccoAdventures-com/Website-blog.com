Programme of Ouzoud

Morning : Departure from Marrakech

• 7:00 AM: Depart early from Marrakech to make the most of your day. The drive to Ouzoud Falls takes about 2.5 to 3 hours (160 km), passing through scenic landscapes of olive groves, Berber villages, and the Atlas Mountains.

• 10:00 AM: Arrive at Ouzoud Falls and start your exploration.

Programme of Ouzoud

Morning : Explore the Falls

• 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Start by hiking down the well-marked path to the base of the falls. Along the way, you’ll encounter several viewpoints offering stunning views of the 110-meter-high waterfalls, which cascade into a series of emerald-green pools. You may also see wild Barbary macaque monkeys in the area.

• 12:00 PM: At the base, you can take a boat ride that brings you closer to the falls. The boatmen usually use small wooden rafts to navigate the pools, providing a unique perspective of the falls.

Afternoon: Lunch and Relaxation

• 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Enjoy a traditional Moroccan lunch at one of the local restaurants near the falls. Most places offer a variety of tagines, salads, and other local dishes. You can dine with a view of the waterfalls in the background.

• 1:30 PM - 2:30 PM: After lunch, take some time to relax by the water, or take a dip in the natural pools if the weather is warm enough. This is a great time to soak in the peaceful surroundings and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.

Afternoon : Explore Surroundings and Return to Marrakech

• 2:30 PM - 3:30 PM: If you’re up for more exploring, you can take a short walk to see the surrounding area, including the olive groves and more panoramic viewpoints of the falls from above.

• 3:30 PM: Begin your journey back to Marrakech.

• 6:30 PM: Arrive back in Marrakech, completing your day trip.
