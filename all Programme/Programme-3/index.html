Merzouga programme

Day 1 : Marrakech to Dades Valley

• Morning: Depart early from Marrakech and drive through the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n’Tichka pass. Along the way, you can stop at the Kasbah Ait Benhaddou, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and continue to Ouarzazate, often called the “Hollywood of Morocco.”

• Afternoon: Continue to Dades Valley, also known as the “Valley of a Thousand Kasbahs.” The scenery is breathtaking, with rugged cliffs and beautiful landscapes.

• Evening: Overnight stay in Dades Valley, where you can explore the local surroundings.

Merzouga programme

Day 2: Dades Valley to Merzouga

• Morning: After breakfast, drive through the Todra Gorge, a spectacular canyon with towering cliffs, and then continue towards Merzouga, the gateway to the Erg Chebbi dunes.

• Afternoon: Arrive in Merzouga and prepare for a camel trek into the Sahara Desert. You’ll ride the camels into the dunes to catch the sunset, an unforgettable experience.

• Evening: Spend the night in a traditional Berber desert camp, where you can enjoy dinner under the stars and experience local music and culture.

Day 3: Merzouga to Marrakech

• Morning: Wake up early to witness the sunrise over the dunes, followed by breakfast at the camp. Afterward, return to Merzouga by camel or 4x4 vehicle.

• Afternoon: Begin the journey back to Marrakech, retracing your steps through the Atlas Mountains. Depending on your pace, you might stop in Ouarzazate or another scenic spot for a break.

• Evening: Arrive back in Marrakech in the evening, completing your desert adventure.
