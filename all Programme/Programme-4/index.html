Ourika Programme

Morning : Departure and Arrival

• 7:00 AM - Departure from Marrakech: Start early to make the most of your day. The drive from Marrakech to Ourika Valley takes about 1.5 hours, so you’ll arrive around 8:30 AM.

• 8:30 AM - Explore Setti Fatma: Head straight to Setti Fatma, the main village in the Ourika Valley. Here, you can explore the local market if it’s a Monday (the day of the weekly souk).

Late Morning: Waterfall Hike

• 9:00 AM - Start the Hike to the Waterfalls: Begin your hike to see the seven waterfalls of Setti Fatma. The hike can take 2-3 hours depending on your pace and how far up you go. Be prepared with good walking shoes and water.

Lunch

• 12:00 PM - Lunch by the River: After the hike, enjoy a leisurely lunch at one of the riverside cafes or restaurants in Setti Fatma. Many places offer traditional Moroccan dishes like tagine or couscous, often with stunning views of the river and mountains.

Afternoon: Cultural and Natural Exploration

• 1:30 PM - Visit a Berber House: After lunch, consider visiting a traditional Berber house. Many families in the area welcome visitors to show their way of life, and it’s a great opportunity to learn about Berber culture and traditions.

• 2:30 PM - Relax by the River or Explore More: If you’re up for more exploring, you can visit nearby villages or take a short walk along the river. Otherwise, you can relax and enjoy the serene environment of the valley.

Late Afternoon: Return to Marrakech

• 4:00 PM - Departure from Ourika Valley: Start heading back to Marrakech. If you want to take a scenic route, you can stop by local cooperatives where women produce argan oil, honey, or visit a saffron farm.

• 5:30 PM - Arrival in Marrakech: Arrive back in Marrakech in the early evening.

Additional Tips :

• What to Bring: Comfortable shoes for hiking, a hat, sunscreen, water, and a camera.

• Best Time to Visit: Spring or fall when the weather is pleasant and the valley is lush and green.

• Local Guide: Hiring a local guide for the hike to the waterfalls can enhance your experience and help you navigate the trail better.
