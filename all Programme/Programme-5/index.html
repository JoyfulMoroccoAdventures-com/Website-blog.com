Zagora Programme

Morning :

• 7:00 AM - Departure from Marrakech: Start early to cover the distance comfortably. The drive to Zagora takes about 6-7 hours, so you want to make the most of the journey.

• 9:00 AM - Stop at Tizi n’Tichka Pass: On your way through the High Atlas Mountains, take a break at the Tizi n’Tichka Pass, the highest road pass in Morocco. Enjoy the stunning views and take some photos.

Tips :

• What to Bring: Comfortable clothing for the desert, sunscreen, sunglasses, a hat, and a scarf to protect against the sun and sand.
